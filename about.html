Who Are We?

Farmerzon farmers group, we a a couple of concerned farmers and we see it is our duty to help other farms like us. Farmers who are not thriving in the farming business. We want them to share the neccessary skill set that each farmer has to empower the other, and all of this can be acheived through the Farmerzon app. While also buying and selling to each other.
